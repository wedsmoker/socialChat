Privacy & Your Data

We're building a social network that respects your privacy. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

What We Collect

  • Account Info: Username, password (hashed), bio, profile picture
  • Your Content: Posts, messages, comments (the stuff you create)
  • Activity Logs: IP addresses, pages visited, timestamps (for security)
  • Session Cookies: So you stay logged in

What We DON'T Do

  • Sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • Track you across other websites
  • Use third-party analytics (no Google Analytics!)
  • Train AI on your posts without permission
  • Share your info with anyone except when legally required

Download Your Data

How to get everything we know about you:

  1. Create an account (if you don't have one)
  2. Go to your profile page
  3. Click "Edit Profile"
  4. Scroll down to "Your Data" section
  5. Click "Download My Data"
  6. Get a JSON file with everything: posts, messages, friends, activity logs

Note: You need an account to download data. We can't export data for anonymous visitors.

Why We Log IP Addresses

We track IP addresses and page visits for two reasons:

  • Security: Detect bots, prevent spam, identify abuse
  • Moderation: Help admins understand suspicious activity

This data stays on our server and is never sold or shared.

Your Rights

  • Access: Download all your data anytime (see above)
  • Delete: Contact admin to permanently delete your account
  • Correct: Edit your profile, posts, and info anytime
  • Know: See exactly what we store about you
